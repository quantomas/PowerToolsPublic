
### Live Server
https://github.com/ritwickdey/vscode-live-server
https://github.com/ritwickdey/vscode-live-server/blob/master/docs/settings.md

1. Open a project and click to Go Live from the status bar to turn the server on/off. 
2. Open in a browser http://localhost:5500 and choose your folder (Default port is 5500)

P.S. Right click on a HTML file from Explorer Window and click on Open with Live Server - doesn't work (checked_SB 06.27.19)
